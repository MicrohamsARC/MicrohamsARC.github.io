## Presentation

### MeshCore: Overview, Comparison, and How-To Demos - Kevin Samson, KI7DEE

Mesh communications is a decentralized system of devices connected to each other in a network, allowing data to be sent and received through multiple paths.

MeshCore is the latest implementation of mesh communications, joining Meshtastic as a method of wireless text messaging without relying on cellular or satellite infrastructure. Kevin will cover how the MeshCore network works and how to get started sending messages over it.

**About the Speaker:** Kevin Samson, KI7DEE, was first licensed in 2016 after a Microsoft coworker introduced him to the hobby. Besides his passion for Parks On The Air and operating whenever and wherever he can, Kevin builds custom drones and is an avid videographer.
