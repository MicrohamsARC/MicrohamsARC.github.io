### June 2025 Field Day
- **Date**: June 27–29, 2025
- **Location**: Camp Freeman: 25821 Monroe Camp Road, Snohomish WA 98290 (47.9798, -121.8866)
- **Details**:

Were you aware that MicroHAMS is again putting on a field day this year?

We are!!! And it's the great site we used last year with BEARONS, just a few minutes Northwest of Monroe. Easy to get to, lots of parking, lots of fun, food and opportunities to get on the air! 

While the actual Field Day "event" starts at 11 AM on Saturday, we'll be headed out to the site at around 11 AM on Friday to start preparing things and having fun. We'll have lots to do and we'd love for you to come any time, and especially if you'd like to come Friday or Saturday to help set up. I'll be sending out more information, and we hope to soon have a web page up, but you can feel free to email me at KB7WSD@gmail.com for more info or to get any questions answered. We look forward to seeing you at the MicroHAMS Field Day 2025!
